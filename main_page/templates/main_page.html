About Me

Hello world! My name is Ryan Miyahara, I was born and raised in San Jose, CA. After graduating from Independence High School, I began college at University of California, Los Angeles. While I started off as a Civil Engineering major with absolutely no interest in coding, my first Computer Science class initiated a personal revelation and from then on, coding became my passion. I am currently in my senior year with an expected completion date in December of 2019.

Outside of coding, I love playing video games with my friends, lifting weights, and finding the freshest memes. My current favorite games are Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (check out my gfycat), Overwatch, Minecraft, and Super Mario Odyssey. I am also a huge fan of terrible coding related jokes.

It's hard to connect to someone through a block of text, but I would love to grab coffee together sometime!
